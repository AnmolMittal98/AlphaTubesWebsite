Tubewell Assembly

Tubewell is a vertical installation of pipes from the ground level to underground reservoirs. The diameter, depth, and thickness of the pipe are determined by the quantity of water required and the region of the bore. Strainers are installed along the length of the bore as per the water cavities. Water through strainers is pulled out of the bore using pre-installed pumps by pipes connected to the pump at one end and the storage at the other end.

PEB Structure

Pre-engineered Building (PEB) Structures are buildings engineered and fabricated at a factory as per the required specifications and pre-prepared drawings. These structures are then transported to the site and assembled with bolted connections. PEBs are usually steel structures, used to build industrial buildings, metro stations, warehouses, factories, canopies, bridges, etc. PEB structures are preferred over conventional steel buildings as they are more economical, and easier and quicker to engineer.
