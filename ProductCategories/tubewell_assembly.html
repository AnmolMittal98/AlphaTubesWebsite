Tubewell Assembly

Our Tubewell Assemblies are designed for efficient water extraction and are built to withstand harsh environmental conditions. These assemblies are made from high-quality materials to ensure durability and long-term performance.

Features:

  • High-quality materials for durability
  • Efficient water extraction
  • Designed for harsh environmental conditions
  • Easy installation and maintenance

Applications:

  • Agricultural irrigation
  • Industrial water supply
  • Domestic water supply

Subproducts

1. Plain Pipes

Plain Pipes are the backbone of any tubewell assembly. They are designed to provide a smooth and continuous flow of water, ensuring efficient extraction. Made from high-quality steel, these pipes are resistant to corrosion and wear, ensuring long-term performance.

2. Slotted Pipes

Slotted Pipes are essential for allowing water to enter the tubewell while keeping debris out. The slots are strategically placed to ensure optimal water flow while preventing clogging. These pipes are ideal for areas with sandy or silty soil conditions.

3. V Screen Pipes

V Screen Pipes are designed to provide superior filtration compared to traditional slotted pipes. The V-shaped slots allow for better water flow and reduce the risk of clogging. These pipes are perfect for areas with fine sediments or high turbidity levels.

4. Housing Clamp

Housing Clamps are used to secure the pipes within the tubewell assembly. They provide a strong and stable connection, ensuring that the pipes remain in place during operation. Made from durable materials, these clamps are resistant to corrosion and wear.

5. H FRAME SUPPORTING GIRDER

The H Frame Supporting Girder provides structural support to the tubewell assembly, ensuring stability and durability. This component is designed to withstand heavy loads and harsh environmental conditions, making it ideal for deep well installations.

6. Borecap

The Borecap is a crucial component that seals the top of the tubewell, preventing debris and contaminants from entering the well. It also provides a secure connection point for the pump and other accessories. Made from high-quality materials, the Borecap ensures long-term protection and reliability.

7. Ring

Rings are used to provide additional support and stability to the tubewell assembly. They are placed at various intervals along the length of the pipes to prevent buckling and ensure a secure fit. Made from durable materials, these rings are resistant to corrosion and wear.

8. Reducer

Reducers are used to connect pipes of different diameters within the tubewell assembly. They ensure a smooth and secure transition, preventing leaks and ensuring efficient water flow. Made from high-quality materials, these reducers are resistant to corrosion and wear.

9. Center Guide

The Center Guide is used to align the pipes within the tubewell assembly, ensuring a straight and secure fit. It prevents misalignment and ensures efficient water flow. Made from durable materials, the Center Guide is resistant to corrosion and wear.

10. Bail Plug

The Bail Plug is used to seal the bottom of the tubewell, preventing debris and contaminants from entering the well. It also provides a secure connection point for the pump and other accessories. Made from high-quality materials, the Bail Plug ensures long-term protection and reliability.
