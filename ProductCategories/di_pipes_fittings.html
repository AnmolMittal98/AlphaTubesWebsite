DI Pipes and Fittings

Our DI Pipes and Fittings are designed for various applications and are built to withstand harsh environmental conditions. These products are made from high-quality materials to ensure durability and long-term performance.

Features:

  • High-quality materials for durability
  • Designed for various applications
  • Built to withstand harsh environmental conditions
  • Easy installation and maintenance

Applications:

  • Water supply systems
  • Sewage systems
  • Irrigation systems

Subproducts

1. A/F or Socket Tee

A/F or Socket Tee is a fitting used to connect three pipes at a 90-degree angle. It is commonly used in water supply and drainage systems. Made from high-quality materials, it ensures a secure and leak-free connection.

2. D Joint

D Joint is a type of pipe fitting used to connect two pipes at a 90-degree angle. It is designed for use in water supply and drainage systems. Made from durable materials, it provides a reliable and long-lasting connection.

3. D/F or Socket Bend

D/F or Socket Bend is a fitting used to change the direction of a pipe by 90 degrees. It is commonly used in water supply and drainage systems. Made from high-quality materials, it ensures a secure and leak-free connection.

4. D/F or Socket Reducer

D/F or Socket Reducer is a fitting used to connect two pipes of different diameters. It is commonly used in water supply and drainage systems. Made from durable materials, it provides a reliable and long-lasting connection.

5. Double Flanged Spool Pipe

Double Flanged Spool Pipe is a type of pipe with flanges on both ends, used for connecting sections of a pipeline. It is commonly used in water supply and drainage systems. Made from high-quality materials, it ensures a secure and leak-free connection.

6. Duckfoot Bend

Duckfoot Bend is a fitting used to change the direction of a pipe by 90 degrees, with a unique shape resembling a duck's foot. It is commonly used in water supply and drainage systems. Made from durable materials, it provides a reliable and long-lasting connection.

7. MZ Mechanical Joint

MZ Mechanical Joint is a type of pipe fitting used to connect two pipes securely without the need for welding or threading. It is commonly used in water supply and drainage systems. Made from high-quality materials, it ensures a secure and leak-free connection.

8. Plain Spigot Pipe

Plain Spigot Pipe is a type of pipe with a smooth, tapered end designed for easy insertion into a socket. It is commonly used in water supply and drainage systems. Made from durable materials, it provides a reliable and long-lasting connection.
